Boxing training

More results, less time spent in the gym.

You don't need to spend hours in the gym every day to get the enviable physique of a fighter. Our workouts are designed for efficiency, meaning you get the most out of each visit without the risk of injury or overtraining. You can burn up to 1,000 calories in each session, and by committing to just 3-5 sessions per week, shred body-fat, improve your overall fitness, and feel more energized.

The "Sweet Science" is real science.

Hard-hitting combos on the bag, challenging circuits, and active rest periods in between rounds turn your body into a calorie-torching, fat-burning machine. High intensity interval training (HIIT) has been shown to increase calorie expenditure and raise your metabolic rate in a shorter amount of time compared to traditional forms of exercise.

Zero experience is needed, and you get 110% from our coaches.

Zero experience is needed to train at KNOCKOUT GYM. Our coaches are highly qualified to guide you on proper form for punches, exercises, and drills. We take the guesswork out of achieving results by giving you the right programming to transform your body. You show up and we'll keep you moving, motivated and making progress especially on the days when you feel like giving up.
